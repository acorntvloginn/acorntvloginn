How to Log In to Your Acorn TV Account
======================================

Follow these simple steps to sign in:

* Open the Acorn TV app or website.
* Select **Sign In**.
* Enter your registered email address.
* Type your account password.
* Select **Sign In** again.
* Complete verification if prompted.
* Access your Acorn TV dashboard and begin streaming.
